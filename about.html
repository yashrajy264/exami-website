The Exami Story

Every year, millions of Indian students face the same nightmare: server crashes during exam registrations, lost documents, multiple application processes, and endless queues. We experienced this firsthand and decided to build the solution India desperately needs.

2023

The Problem

Identified the massive inefficiencies in India's examination system affecting 50+ million students annually.

2024

The Vision

Conceptualized a unified platform with unique 8-digit IDs, eliminating redundant processes and server crashes.

2025

The Future

Building India's first comprehensive examination ecosystem with AI-powered security and seamless user experience.
