Everything you need to know about Exami

Exami is a unified platform for examination infrastructure in India that addresses critical issues like server downtimes, document scrutiny problems, and admit card mismanagement. We handle the pre and post phases of examinations - from application to results - replacing the outdated infrastructure that can't handle high traffic volumes.

Our LinkedIn Easy Apply-style process has three simple steps: Step 1 - Basic info automatically fetched from your Exami profile created during onboarding; Step 2 - Additional questions or documents required by the institution; Step 3 - Review and payment. It's that simple!

Every student gets a unique 8-digit alphanumeric Exami ID (similar to PAN system) that works across all institutions and examinations. This single ID eliminates the hassle of creating multiple accounts and reduces paperwork for every application.

Institutions need minimal training to pivot to Exami. Simply login to your dashboard, create an exam announcement, fill relevant data, build forms with drag-and-drop blocks, choose payment methods, and publish directly on our platform. You can also link exam centers and manage everything from one dashboard.

The mobile app includes in-app drive functionality for document storage, document scanner feature, and a planner-style calendar that fetches all notifications and important dates. Students can also create custom events like revision schedules. We also offer Google account integration for seamless experience.

We respect user privacy completely. Data provided by students for specific applications cannot be viewed by us, and institutions can only access the data that students explicitly provide for their applications. All data is encrypted and stored securely with bank-grade security measures.
